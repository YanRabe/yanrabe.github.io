# Yan Rabefaniraka - Personal E-Portfolio

Welcome to the repository for my personal e-portfolio and academic website, hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://yanrabe.github.io). This site is built using [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and is based on the [al-folio](https://github.com/alshedivat/al-folio) theme.

## About This Portfolio

This website serves as a comprehensive showcase of my academic journey, professional development, and personal initiatives as an engineering student in Toulouse, France. 

The site is organized into several key areas:
* **Engineering Course:** Details about my academic curriculum and engineering studies.
* **Career Development:** Insights into my professional goals, experiences, and growth.
* **International Mobility:** Experiences and perspectives from my international academic/professional mobility.
* **Projects & Publications:** A collection of technical projects, research, and papers I've worked on.
* **Sustainability & Civic Engagement:** My involvement in sustainable initiatives and civic duties.
* **Sport & Other Activities:** A look into my extracurricular activities and hobbies.
* **CV:** My complete, up-to-date resume.
